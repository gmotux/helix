Campaign Truth

What Kallisto Was Actually Doing

Public Face
A private biomedical and resilience firm running HELIX as a real summit, donor event, and clinical-branding exercise.
Hidden Program
A long-running human stability pipeline built around rare outlier cases: people who stayed coherent under shock, pain, isolation, contradiction, or impossible sensor behavior better than they should.
Building A
Screening, interviews, reroutes, first-pass testing, and private handling under summit cover.
Building B
Recheck, draw, packet control, release authorization, and freight staging for material and people that were meant to move onward.
Orpheus Annex
The real restricted site: containment, live source work, stronger instrumentation, and the rooms Kallisto never intended normal staff or summit guests to see.
Real Objective
Identify which people remain useful, stable, and controllable when ordinary systems fail, then move them or their data deeper into the network before anyone outside Kallisto can read the pattern.
14:23

What Physically Happened

Window
Kallisto opened a scheduled protected handoff window between the Westwood campus and Orpheus Annex.
Visible Onset
Chest-felt boom, pressure drop, minor structural shudder, mains trip, then dim red emergency lighting.
Trigger
A contained source case at Orpheus Annex, listed in restricted records as OA-SC-17 "NEMEA", destabilized during that handoff. The failure propagated across the linked facilities instead of staying local.
Result
Mass death, violent mutation, coherent awakenings in rare outliers, and a residue field that later becomes the mist problem.
Pre-Rupture Reality

What Kallisto Knew Before It Broke

Weak Anomalies Existed

Kallisto had years of disputed records: sealed-room pressure drift, impossible survivor timing, mutually incompatible witness statements, sensors disagreeing around specific patients, and recovery cases that should not have stabilized.

No Public Proof

Before 14:23 these effects were weak enough to dismiss as bad instruments, bad memory, contamination, fraud, or trauma.

What They Could Measure

Blood markers, medication response, decision speed under contradiction, reaction lag, recovery quality, and whether a person stayed coherent when ordinary scripts stopped working.

What They Compared It To

By summit day, Kallisto was quietly indexing unusual results against one Annex source file: OA-SC-17 "NEMEA". Staff did not know what that case really was, only that anything close to its pattern got retained.

Why The Secrecy

If Kallisto published weak anomaly data and private human-screening work together, regulators and sponsors would shut the whole program down before it ever reached a hard conclusion.

PC Significance

Why The PCs Survived As Themselves

Stable Outliers

The PCs fit Kallisto's pattern before the rupture: unusual resilience, usable stress behavior, and measurable markers Kallisto had already started screening for.

Not Safe, Just Rare

Most exposed people died, ruptured, or lost identity. The PCs crossed the same event and remained self-directed. That is the exceptional result Kallisto was looking for, but had not yet proven.

How Abilities Work

Awakened effects are coherent mind-body interactions with the altered field left by the rupture. They are real, costly, and limited, not freeform spellcasting.

Internal Split

Chen wants continuity and controllable survivors. Vale wants usable adaptation. Serrat wants containment and leverage. Patel and Sorrell want clean answers from unstable data. They cooperated before 14:23 and will not stay aligned after it.

5e Field Items

Chimera Triage Injector

Consumable prototype

Single-use spring injector in a matte black sleeve stamped with the Chimera mark.

Use: Bonus action on yourself, or action on a willing creature within 5 feet.

Effect: The target gains 6 temporary hit points, immediately ends the frightened condition if it came from shock or pain, and has advantage on Constitution saving throws for 1 minute. If the target is at 0 hit points, it instead rises to 1 hit point and also gains the temporary hit points.

Crash: When the minute ends, the target makes a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, it gains one level of exhaustion.

Table Use: Military emergency chemistry, not a healing potion. It keeps someone moving through one bad fight and then makes them pay for it.

Chimera Aster-9

Martial ranged weapon, firearm, prototype

Polymer-frame 9mm sidearm with a dull ceramic slide, flush optic notch, and a grip sensor that glows red when the wrong hand closes on it.

Damage: 2d6 piercing.

Range: 40/120.

Magazine: 12 shots. Reloading with a fresh magazine takes an action.

Operator Lock: A keyed user fires it normally. An unkeyed user gets a dead trigger, a red grip-light, and an audible lock chirp that can be heard out to the next room.

Seizure Rule: A creature can bypass the lock only by spending 10 minutes with tinker's tools or thieves' tools and succeeding on a DC 15 Dexterity check. On a failure, the pistol hard-locks until the end of a short rest.

Mist Field

Usable Rules

Building A State

Inside: no full ambient mist field yet.

Inside effects: short death-plumes, localized anomaly rooms, and contaminated air only where a room has gone bad.

Outside: the campus already holds a heavier residue field because too many altered bodies, dead zones, and active anomalies overlap there.

Death-Plume Rule

Whenever a changed human dies, it exhales a short black cloud in a 5-foot radius.

The cloud usually fades by the end of the next round unless nearby anomalies or destructive awakened output keep feeding it.

Area Cleansing

An area is mostly cleansed when hostile mutants are dead, active anomalies are shut down, and any remaining changed people are either calm, moved, or stabilized.

Killing a sympathetic changed person also works, but it is the brutal option.

Power Direction

Destructive awakened output: local residue lasts longer, thickens faster, and makes the room harsher.

Protective, healing, light, or calming output: local residue thins faster and anomalies become easier to shut down.

Mist Exposure

Make a Constitution save when a PC is within 5 feet of a changed human as it dies, enters heavy exterior residue, starts a turn near a major anomaly, or uses destructive awakened output inside active mist.

DCs: 10 death-plume, 12 ambient mist, 14 anomaly surge or heavy district. Add +2 after destructive awakened output.

Mist Marks

Fail: gain 1 Mist Mark. Fail by 5 or more: also take 1d4 poison damage.

1: cough, sting, watering eyes.

2: disadvantage on next Perception or Concentration check.

3: speed reduced by 10 feet.

4: poisoned until cleared.

Clearing Mist Marks

Remove 1 mark with clean shelter, water, and 10 minutes of rest; with medical treatment; or with cleansing/protective magic.

Clear all marks with a full rest in a clean zone or after a successful coherence-style cleansing rite.

Table Use

Run Building A as the first place the party learns the rule.

No pre-event document in Building A should already explain the rule. The party learns it from fresh notes, witness accounts, death-plumes, and post-14:23 observations.
