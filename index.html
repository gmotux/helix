Welcome to The Gloaming Line

Your Session 0 begins in Boston MA, USA, April 2026. Your character has agreed to enter a restricted clinical program being run during HELIX week at Kallisto Biomedical's facilities. They are not walking into this blindly by accident; they had a reason to say yes, and that reason needs to matter.

Before you continue, decide what your character thinks this invitation means. Is it money, treatment, leverage, access, prestige, obligation, fear, a favor, a private lead, or a chance they cannot afford to lose? Decide who they care about, what they are hiding, what they stand to lose, and what they hope Boston will give them.

Write whatever helps you inhabit them. Then, when the forms appear, answer everything in character. Do not optimize. Do not answer like a player trying to beat a quiz. Answer like someone making real choices with limited time, incomplete context, and something personal at stake.

Before You Begin

Decide why your character accepted this invitation, what they hope to gain from it, and what personal ties or unresolved pressures they carry into Boston.

Reason Give them a strong reason to say yes: money, treatment, leverage, access, loyalty, fear, ambition, curiosity, or duty.
